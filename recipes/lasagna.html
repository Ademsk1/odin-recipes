Tasty Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Beef Mince
  • Tomatoes
    1. Cook beef mince
    2. Add tomatoes to pot
    3. Boil lasagna sheets
    4. Layer the beef and tomatoes with the lasagna sheets and cook in the oven
